Kraus Moore Dinner Planning

Here are some dinners we like!

One bowl: Veggie pasta (tomato sauce or pesto), Egg bowl, Sesame noodles with veggies. Salads: Taco salad, Tomato mozzarella salad, Salad with tortellini, Chicken or steak salad. Diner style (see veggie sides): Breakfast for dinner, Regular steak, BBQ chicken, Roast chicken, Marinated flank steak, Pot roast (potatoes and carrots). Veggie sides: Roasted asparagus, Roasted broccoli/cauliflower, Steamed green beans, Chard with pancetta, Steamed artichokes, Roasted root vegetables, Sweet potatoes, Roasted new potatoes, Balsamic roasted Brussels, Roasted peppers. Hearty soup/stew: Minestrone, Ratatouille, Beef stew, Chili. Other: Soup, bread, cheeses, Naan or Boboli pizzas, Stir fry veggies and chicken over rice.
